How to use the app
  • Clock In/Out: Click the button to clock in or out.
  • History: View the history of clock in/out times.
  • Admin: View the admin page. This displays the totals.
  • Help: View the help page.
Definitions
  • Period Total: The total hours worked for the current period.
  • YTD Total: The total hours worked for the current year.
